Facts about COVID-19 in India

Some Facts...

  • India stands second after USA for the highest number of positive cases reported in the world and even for the highest number of deaths.


  • In India there is just 1 bed avilable per 2000 people, which creates huge amount of shortage of beds which makes the life of a covid patient miserable. Even oxygen concentrators are very less compared to required amount, which is a big set back.


  • People of elderly age like 60-70 and 50-60 years are getting infected the most.Covid is less seen in teenagers and children compared to adults.


  • India administered over 182 million vaccine doses for the coronavirus as of May 15th.With the onset of second wave since march, the central govt increased funding to boost vaccination production.
