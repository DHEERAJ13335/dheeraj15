Preventive Measures..

Precautions taken against COVID in our country

In order to defeat the virus, our country is been working hard from more than a year. There are many protocols followed by the people of our country throughout this pandemic.

Lockdown

Lockdown is a restriction policy for people or community to stay where they are,usually due to specific risks to themselves or to others.

On 24th march 2020, government ordered a nationwide lockdown as a preventive measure against covid-19 pandemic in India.During lockdown all the activities will be completely stopped except the essenial services.

First lockdown was for around 3 months and again now due to the second wave of the covid government has again announced lockdown for 15 days

Vaccination Drive

India began administration of COVID-19 vaccines on 16th january 2021.Two vaccines recieved approval in use in India namely covishield and covaxin.

Firstly vaccines were provided for the frontline workers and after them to the elderly people above the age of 45.Now vaccines are even provided to teenage people

Everybody should get themselves vaccinated properly and ensure safety against the covid-19 pandemic.

Self safety

In order to ensure our own safety, people should follow some precautions and avoid large public gatherings such as ceremonies, election rallies, family functions and many more.

We should wear a mask that covers our nose and mouth, should stay at a min distance of 6ft from others.We should wash our hands often with soap and water, use hand sanitizer and keep ourselves clean.

If we follow all the safety measures and stay safe, we will have the min risk of getting infected.
