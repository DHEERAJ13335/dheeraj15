Frontline Workers around Us

There are many frontline workers who are working in various sectors. Mainly they are

Doctors/health staff

They have critical roles in diagnosis, containment and treatement and their commitment to treat despite increased personal risks in essential for a successful public health response. Doctors are working overtime risking their life in order to control te virus.

Police/security personnel

They enforce the lockdown through movement restriction, giving assistance to the vulnerable during crisis.They trace the contact and use investigation and intelligence skill.Security staff are the people who respond first in any critical situation.

Volunteers

Volunteers colect data and information about the disaster and spread awareness in prople in order to control the pandemic.They can work in health, communication, enterpreneural and in other sectors.
